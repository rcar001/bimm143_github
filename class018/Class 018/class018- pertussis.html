Background

Pertussis (Whooping Cough) is a common lung infection caused by the bacteria B. pertussis. Most incompetent individuals will clear the infection. However, this infection can be deadly in infants.

As the Center for Disease Control (CDC) tracks the number of reported cases int he US:

Link: https://www.cdc.gov/pertussis/php/surveillance/pertussis-cases-by-year.html?CDC_AAref_Val=https://www.cdc.gov/pertussis/surv-reporting/cases-by-year.html

Q. Add some major milestones including the first wP vaccine (1946), the aP vaccine(1996), and COVID pandemic start at (2020).

Blue: wP vaccine introduced.

Red: Public opinion of vaccine declines and we see susceptible populations infection rates rise. Notably, around the 1996 time point, we have the rise of public access to internet and spread of misinformation.

Green: Covid_19 Pandemic

aP induced immunity weans faster than wP immunity. It appears that immunizations which stimulate a more robust immune response, due to the full set of inactivated components present, result in stronger immunological memory.

##CMI-PB Project (Computational Models of Immunity - Pertussis Boost)

This project aims to provide the scientific community with this very information.
